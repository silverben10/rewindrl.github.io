About Us


Rewind Gaming began in September simply as a group of friends looking to play with other people. It grew steadily from there, focussing on holding Rocket League tournaments for people both in the server and outside it. We host 3v3 (and now 1v1) events every single week, so make sure to join if you want to take part!
